About Me

I’m Lintle Mofelehetsi, a multimedia journalist passionate about uncovering truths, spotlighting unheard voices, and using storytelling as a tool for impact. My work spans investigative reporting, audio production, and photojournalism.

I have always been interested in investigative journalism, and in 2024, I enrolled from Limkokwing University of Creative Technology with a Associate Degree in Journalism and Media. My studies focused on Investigative Journalism, Podcasting & Audio Features, Visual Storytelling & Photo Essays, Culture, Politics, and Human Rights particularly the political and historical context of the Lesotho.

My Journey

I grew up fascinated by the power of storytelling, from traditional Sesotho folktales to global news. This curiosity led me to journalism, where I blend words, audio, and visuals to bring stories to life.

Mission & Values

I believe in journalism that is ethical, empathetic, and fearless. My mission is to amplify marginalized perspectives, uncover truths, and tell stories that matter — with dignity and depth.

What I'm Working On

I'm currently producing a mini-documentary series on climate justice in Southern Africa and launching a new investigative podcast called “Whispers in the Wind.”
